Reguna

Beyond dips, the country keyed in on Cocktail Weenies, which were the most popular Super Bowl food in Colorado, Nebraska, and much of the Midwest, dominating in Wisconsin, Minnesota, Michigan, and Indiana.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese (Neufchatel)
